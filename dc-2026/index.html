Twilight Monument Walk ✨
✅ The Monument Visit
This is YOUR monument experience: April sunset is ~7:40pm. After dinner the monuments are dramatically lit. Seeing them for the first time at night is magical — open 24/7, free.
Classic loop (~1.2 mi): Uber to Lincoln Memorial → Lincoln steps → Reflecting Pool → Vietnam Veterans → Korean War → WWII → Washington Monument (lit). Uber home from Monument area. ~1 hour total. (8:30pm ET = 5:30pm body clock — kids have energy.)
Short loop (~0.8 mi): Walk from Riggs to Mall → Washington Monument → WWII Memorial briefly → Uber home. Easy on tired legs.
Weather contingency: If significant rain on Day 2, swap with Day 3 (American History + NMAAHC, entirely indoors)
